Privacy Policy

Effective Date: October 2024

1. Information We Collect

Obsidian Funded collects personal data necessary to provide a premium trading experience. This include identifiers such as your full name, email address, residential address, and date of birth.

We also collect technical data including IP addresses, browser types, and device identification to ensure the security of our terminal environment and prevent unauthorized access.

  • Registration Data: Identity and contact details.
  • Financial Data: Transaction history and withdrawal information.
  • KYC Verification: Official government-issued documentation.

2. How We Use Data

Your data is processed to facilitate access to the proprietary trading environment and to execute payouts efficiently. We utilize automated systems to monitor performance and compliance with our trading rules.

Marketing communications are only sent to users who have explicitly opted into our premium news alerts and updates.

3. Data Security

We implement military-grade encryption protocols for data at rest and in transit. Our infrastructure is hosted on secure, high-availability clusters with 24/7 monitoring.

Access to personal data is strictly limited to authorized personnel who require it to perform essential support or compliance functions.

4. Cookies & Tracking

Our terminal uses session cookies to maintain authentication and ensure a consistent user experience. We use analytical tools to understand how our users interact with the dashboard, allowing us to optimize performance.

Users can manage cookie preferences through their browser settings, though disabling essential cookies may impact terminal functionality.

5. Your Rights

Under international data protection laws, you maintain the following rights over your personal information:

  • Right of access to your stored data.
  • Right to rectification of inaccurate records.
  • Right to erasure ('right to be forgotten').
  • Right to data portability.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact our compliance department via the secure support channel in your dashboard.
